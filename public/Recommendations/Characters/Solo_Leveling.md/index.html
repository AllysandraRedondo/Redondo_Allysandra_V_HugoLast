Main Characters#

Sung Jin-Woo:
The protagonist who starts as the weakest E-rank Hunter but gains the ability to level up after inheriting the mysterious System, eventually becoming the Shadow Monarch.

Sung Jin-Ah:
Jin-Woo’s younger sister, a caring and supportive family member who studies hard for her future.

Sung Il-Hwan:
Jin-Woo’s father, who disappeared during a dungeon raid and later returned with mysterious powers.

Shadows (Jin-Woo’s Summons)#

Igris:
A loyal knight-like Shadow soldier, previously a Red Knight in a dungeon. One of Jin-Woo’s strongest and most reliable summons.

Beru:
A former ant king turned Shadow. Extremely powerful and fiercely loyal to Jin-Woo.

Tusk:
A high-level mage Shadow, formerly an orc priest. Provides magical support.

Hunters from Korea#

Cha Hae-In:
Korea’s only female S-rank Hunter and a member of the Hunters Guild. She becomes Jin-Woo’s love interest and eventually his wife.

Go Gun-Hee:
The president of the Korean Hunters Association, an influential and wise leader who recognizes Jin-Woo’s potential.

Yoo Jin-Ho:
Jin-Woo’s loyal friend and party member, the heir to a wealthy conglomerate.

International Hunters#

Thomas Andre:
An American S-rank Hunter and one of the strongest hunters in the world, known as the “Goliath.”

Liu Zhigang:
A Chinese S-rank Hunter, renowned for his immense power and status.

Christopher Reed:
An American S-rank Hunter who falls victim to the Monarchs.
